the chilean saints

1.- ¿How many Chilean saints are there?

Chile actually has two saints,
Santa Teresa de Los Andes and San Alberto Hurtado.
They are the only ones who have made it to the end of the path of the canonization process.
Behind them are our blessed, who are four:
Laura Vicuña, Ceferino Namuncurá, José Agustín Fariña y María Crescencia Pérez

2.- definition of Chilean saints

The saints in Chile are a sign of a faith that bore fruit and was projected in life.
The renewed interest in the history of Chile that the Bicentennial has aroused has also stirred
the memory of the saints and saints who have left their mark on said history.
